# Dashboard Insights

## Dashboard 1 — Executive Overview

### Key Insights

- The platform achieved a conversion rate of 37%.
- Basic members account for 35.36% of total users.
- Evening is the highest-performing period for conversions.
- The largest funnel drop-off occurs between Cart and Purchase.

---

## Dashboard 2 — Conversion Analysis

### Key Insights

- High-loyalty users achieve conversion rates above 55%.
- High-engagement users reach nearly 58% conversion.
- High recommendation scores generate conversion rates close to 73%.
- Basic members unexpectedly outperform premium tiers.

---

## Dashboard 3 — Engagement Analysis

### Key Insights

- Cart activity is the strongest behavioral predictor.
- Wishlist users account for over 76% of purchases.
- Longer viewing durations correlate with higher conversion.
- Cart → Purchase remains the largest funnel bottleneck.

---

## Dashboard 4 — Recommendation System Performance

### Key Insights

- High recommendation scores exceed 70% conversion.
- Top-ranked recommendations drive the most conversions.
- High-affinity users significantly outperform others.
- Ranking stability strongly impacts recommendation success.
